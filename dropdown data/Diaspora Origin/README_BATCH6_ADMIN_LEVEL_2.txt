What this batch does:
- Adds a selective, ancestry-focused ADM2 seed for the federal-model countries.

- Uses the practical second-level unit that best fits each country:
  * United States -> county / county equivalent
  * Canada -> census division / regional district / county-equivalent division

Important modeling note:
This batch is intentionally selective, not exhaustive.
It is designed for origin/diaspora mapping and community-lineage capture, not for complete nationwide gazetteer coverage.

Why selective:
- Federal countries do not share one perfectly uniform ADM2 logic across all states/provinces.
- Your project is ancestry-oriented, so a targeted seed is safer than forcing a false impression of completeness.
- These rows are intended to support the next ADM3 build around actual towns/cities/settlements and diaspora-community mapping.
